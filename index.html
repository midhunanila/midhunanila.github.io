About Me

I am a PhD candidate specializing in Computational Biophysics at the Polish Academy of Sciences. My work focuses on molecular dynamics simulations of intrinsically disordered proteins (IDPs), exploring complex biomolecular mechanisms using physics-based approaches.

Research Interests

  • Molecular Dynamics Simulations
  • Intrinsically Disordered Proteins
  • Membrane Biophysics & Protein Interactions
  • Coarse-Grained Modeling & IDP Aggregation
